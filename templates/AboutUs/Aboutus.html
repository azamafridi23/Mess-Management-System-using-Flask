Mess Management System of GIKI

  • The system would allow students and Mess SuperVisor, to register with their institute credentials and authenticate themselves to access the system.
  • The system would provide a platform for the mess staff to upload daily menus for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. It could include options for different dietary preferences and special meals for students with allergies or dietary restrictions.
  • The system would generate bills for meal bookings and allow users to make payments online or through their institute accounts
  • The system would allow users to provide feedback on the quality of meals and overall dining experience. This feedback could be used to improve the menu, service, and facilities.
